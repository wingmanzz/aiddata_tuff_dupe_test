Title: Minister: (China expresses satisfaction over speed of development work on CPEC: Bakhtiar)

Snippet: Minister for Planning, Development and Reform, Makhdoom Khusro Bakhtiar has said that China has expressed satisfaction over the speed of development work on China Pakistan Economic Corridor.

Talking to private news channel, he said Railway line project 'ML-1' is the most important project for development of the country.

Body: 
To a question, he said all preparations have been made to inaugurate Rashakai economic zone during next month. The minister said the government is developing Gwadar on priority as shipping hub of the country.
