Title: An MoU signed between PBIT, Saudi and Chinese Companies - Press Note issued by Punjab Board of Investment and Trade

Snippet: December 30, 2019 (PPI-OT)

Following is the text of press note issued by Punjab Board of Investment and Trade (PBIT)

Body: 
Quote

A tri party MoU signed between PBIT, a Saudi Company - Chemicals Products Co Ltd and a Chinese company Shandong Reach Supply Chain Management Co. Ltd. They will do investment in agriculture sector. The Company will share details of their company with regards to their expertise in development of green houses.
