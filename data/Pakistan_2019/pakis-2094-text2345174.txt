Title: Minister: (Pakistan, China to sign preliminary design of ML-1 project: Minister)

Snippet: Pakistan and China will sign the preliminary design of ML-1 project during the upcoming visit of Prime Minister Imran Khan to Beijing.

This was stated by Minister for Railways Sheikh Rashid Ahmed while talking to Vice-Governor of China's province Hubei, Huang Chuping here on Friday.

Body: 
He said completion of Peshawar to Karachi Main Line 1 project will bring revolution to railways in Pakistan. He said the projects of China-Pakistan Economic Corridor have ushered in a golden era between the two countries.
