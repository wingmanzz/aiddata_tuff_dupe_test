Title: Ch Energy Eng (03996) signs Pakistan power station contract

Snippet: [ET Net News Agency, 1 April 2019] China Energy Engineering Corporation Limited (03996)said its subsidiary China Gezhouba Group Stock Company Limited (Gezhouba) has entered intoa contract for Mohmand power station in Pakistan with a consortium.

The contents of the project include construction of civil engineering projects such as rock-filled dam with a concrete face, two diversion tunnels, etc. The total installed capacity of the project is 800MW, and the annual power generation capacity is 3,035GWh.

Body: 
The contracted amount of the project is equivalent to RMB12.475 billion, of which RMB8.708 billion in equivalent is attributable to Gezhouba. The contract term is 2,070 days commencing from the date of commencement of construction. (RC)
