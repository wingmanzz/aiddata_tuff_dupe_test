Title: RIM PETROCHEMICALS NO.2963 PET and RAW MATERIALS Purified Terephthalic Acid / Monoethylene Glycol

Snippet: Prices for purified terephthalic acid (PTA) on a bonded warehouse basis were in the range of $610-630/mt on Wednesday, flat from the previous day. China domestic prices inched higher by Yuan 5/mt to Yuan 4,860-4,880/mt. The China domestic market found support from a moderate rise in PTA futures prices. Spot discussions took place at Yuan 4,860-4,880/mt.

Prices for ethylene glycol (EG) on a CFR China basis rose to $545-546/mt on Wednesday, up $5/mt from the previous day. China domestic prices leveled off at Yuan $4,600-4,650/mt. The CFR China market was pushed up by the tighter supply outlook amid lower operating rates at domestic EG facilities. On a CFR China basis, talks were heard at $545-546/mt. In the China domestic market, trade levels centered on Yuan 4,625/mt.
