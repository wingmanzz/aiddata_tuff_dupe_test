Title: Pakistan to establish authority to further support CPEC construction: official

Snippet: BEIJING, July 16 (Xinhua) â€“ Pakistan plans to set up an authority to further support the construction of China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC), according to Makhdum Khusro Bakhtyar, Minister of Planning, Development & Reform addressed at a press conference recently.

It is noted that the authority to be established is under the Ministry of Planning, Development & Reform, which is to further support of multi sectoral coordination and coordinate efforts for development and implementation of CPEC plans, according to the minister.

Body: 
Due to the endeavors of the joint efforts by China and Pakistan, CPEC has now entered into the second phase where the focus will be laid on industrial and agricultural cooperation, and the development of Gwadar Port as well as socio-economic development, said the minister.

Specifically, Gwardar Port will be developed into a smart port city of international standard, according to the minister, to enable Gwardar to emerge as the leading trading port in the region.

The minister also revealed that the Pakistani government plans to upgrade line 1 railway project under the CPEC, which will effectively enhance the country's transportation capacity and economic development after its completion. (Edited by Jiang Feifan)
