Title: Akbar Askani inaugurates Fishermen Floating Jatty in Gadani

Snippet: Asianet Pakistan Private Limited

QUETTA: Provincial Minister for Fisheries Haji Akbar Askani on Friday inaugurated Gadani Fishermen Floating Jatty added the China-Pakistan joint venture for provincial development would be succeeded. "The provincial government has fulfilled the prime demand of fishermen community of coastal city Gadani, the fruits of CPEC has commenced in Balochistan that would change the fortune of people of Balochistan.

Body: 
" Minister Akbar Askani said while addressing the inaugurating ceremony. He added, CM Balochistan has been utilizing his earnest efforts to make successful CPEC and Pak-China joint venture for provincial development has strengthened our friendship with China.

Talking on the energy projects in Balochistan Akbar Askani said, progress has been intensified over 5000mw Hubco power project which would fill the energy need of Balochistan. Addressing the ceremony Chief Executive of China-Pakistan Hub Generation Company Mr.

Zhao Yaggang said, the project would generate electricity for 4 million homes while the energy projects were helpful for Pakistan's economy, "The Gadani Floating Jetty would be completed with the cost of 400,000 dollars and would be Pakistan's first floating Jetty,." Mr.

Zhao said added progress underway for the educational development of Alana Goth while many play grounds were already constructed for the youth. He pledged that local population is engaged in technical programs and they would be provided jobs in various development projects.
