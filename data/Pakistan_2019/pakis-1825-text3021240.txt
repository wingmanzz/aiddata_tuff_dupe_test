Title: Sinomach - Financing agreed for CMEC project in Pakistan

Snippet: Release date - 10012019

Zhang Chun, chairman of China Machinery Engineering Corporation, speaks at a signing ceremony for the financing agreements needed for the TEL 1x330MW power project in Pakistan, on Dec 20, 2018.

Body: 
A signing ceremony for the financing agreements necessary to build the TEL 1x330MW power project in Pakistan took place on Dec 20, 2018.

China Machinery Engineering Corporation (CMEC), a subsidiary of Sinomach, as the contractor and an investor in the project, signed a series of financing agreements with Chinese and Pakistani financial institutes headed by China Development Bank and Habib Bank Limited.

The new power station is considered to be one of the signature projects of the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor.
