Title: Signing of the Cooperation Framework Agreement at PBIT - Press Note by Punjab Board of Investment and Trade

Snippet: Asianet Pakistan Private Limited

April 04, 2019 (PPI-OT)

Body: 
Following is the text of press note issued by Punjab Board of Investment and Trade (PBIT)

Quote

Recognizing Pakistan's potential as the 4th largest cotton producer of the world Chinese investors announced the garments and texile industry investment in Pakistan in Punjab from China. Signing of the Cooperation Framework Agreement took place between the following companies In the presence of Minsiter Industries Commerce and Investments Punjab and Chinese counsul General Longdin Bin.

1. China Railway 20 Co. Pakistan (Private) Limited

2. Shanghai Yuanyi Industry Company Limited

3. Pak China Investment Company Limited

4. The Punjab Board of Investment and Trade
