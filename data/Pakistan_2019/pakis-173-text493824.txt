Title: Pakistani students attend Intelligent Power Distribution Technology training in China

Snippet: The first batch of Pakistani students has successfully completed training at a workshop “Intelligent Power Distribution Technology” held at Shandong Industrial and Vocational College in china.

A group of nine Pakistani students from Infinity Engineering College (ISE) attended 20 days training workshop.

Body: 
The Infinity School of Engineering (ISE) focuses on imparting vocational and technical training to school dropouts, unemployed youth, existing workers, trainers, and working professionals in the manufacturing, agriculture, and engineering sectors.

Vice President Zhao Hongjun welcoming the students in training and introduced the basic situation of the college and the relevant arrangements of the training program as well as teachers and teaching.

He wished all the students a success in learning and becoming the messenger of China-Pakistan friendly exchanges, promoting the further exchange of students and teachers between the two universities after attending training on intelligent Power Distribution Technology.

Dean Ma Guangting and Vice President Zhao Hongjun made a special trip to the training center to learn about the training of the trainees.

The seminar participants visited the Linyi Qi Culture Museum and the Ancient Car Horse Museum.

A total of 26 institutions and enterprises in 12 countries and regions have established cooperative relations and gradually formed an equal, inclusive, mutually beneficial and active educational cooperation situation.
