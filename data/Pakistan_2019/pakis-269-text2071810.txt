Title: Chinese to provide technical expertise in the textile sector: Dawood

Snippet: Advisor to Prime Minister for Commerce Abdul Razak Dawood says a free trade agreement has been signed with China and he will visit China to meet Chinese Trade Minister soon. Addressing a news conference in Islamabad he said a Chinese non-government organization will visit Pakistan next month to provide technical expertise in the textile sector. He said Chinese companies intend to manufacture textile products in Pakistan.

The Advisor said the government's policies for the uplift of economic situation are on the right track and economy cannot be strengthened without enhancing exports.

Body: 
Speaking on the occasion, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Yao Jing said China has opened its ninety percent market for Pakistani products. Chinese companies are relocating their supply chain. He said with one percent relocation to Pakistan, the country can expect an investment of 600 million dollars.
