Title: Lashkari urges special grants for research and innovation programs

Snippet: Being a heart of China Pakistan Economic Corridor, the least developed province of the country deserves special grants in developing research and innovation programs for provincial youth. Lashkari Raisani said.

'Provincial Universities looking toward Federal government regarding special grants in order to foster research and innovation in Balochistan following the multi-billion dollars Chinese investment.' Raisani said on Friday.

Body: 
He shared these views while addressing a laptop distribution ceremony held at sub-campus of University of Balochistan in Mastung.

Terming Balochistan as strategic hub of the region the senior leader of BNP Mengal said, following the importance of geographical importance of our province, provincial youth needs to pursue higher studies including get knowledge of Information Technology,

'Tough the internet and technology have eased public life but one cannot disagree from the importance of books hence our youth should build connection with book reading.' Raisani said stressed upon youth to start reading books.

He further said, education was the only sources that can steer Balochistan out from current turmoil therefore people living Balochistan needs to educate their children.

Addressing the ceremony Vice Chancellor UoB Muhammad Anwar Panezai has lauded the performance of Mastung sub-campus pledged to open new educational departments in the varsity.
