Title: Five-point formula with China termed beneficial for Pakistan

Snippet: Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf MNA Nusrat Wahid said Sunday that the five points formula with China will be very helpful for the future and prosperity of Pakistan.

Necessary funds have been allocated for climate change, tourism corridor, elimination of poverty. Co-operation with China in the field of education, health, agriculture is of greater importance," she said in a statement.

Body: 
She further said that the cooperation between the two countries on IT, infrastructure, railway and energy is essential and will be further expanded. In view of the global situation and trade imbalances, the role of BRI International Co-operation, partnership, expansion of communications will be beneficial for the countries and will be a land mark.

Nusrat said that China and Pakistan were entering into the 2nd phase of CPEC and through this, the social and economic prosperity would be enhanced as the Pakistan government was focusing on such a particular issue.

The international community should take full advantage of investment opportunities available in Pakistan, as the PTI government is giving full support to foreign investors by providing them all the requisite facilities in this respect," she said.
