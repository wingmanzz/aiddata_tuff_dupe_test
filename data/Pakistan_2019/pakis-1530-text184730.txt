Title: Dr. Firdous thanks China for endorsing Pakistan's stance on oppressed Kashmiris

Snippet: Special Assistant to PM on Information and Broadcasting Dr. Firdous Ashiq Awan says Prime Minister Imran Khan's ongoing visit to China will further strengthen strategic partnership between the two countries and promote bilateral cooperation in economy and investment.

In series of tweets on Tuesday, she said Prime Minister will meet Chinese Persident Xi Jinping and Premier Li Keqiang and discuss with them the grave human rights violations in occupied Kashmir.

Body: 
She thanked China for endorsing Pakistan's stance on the oppressed Kashmiris.

Firdous Ashiq Awan said the Prime Minister will also apprise the Chinese leadership about the government's historic decisions regarding implementation of the CPEC projects. She said we thank China for endorsing Pakistan's stance on the oppressed Kashmiris.
