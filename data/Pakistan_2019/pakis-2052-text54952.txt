Title: Minister: (OBOR to become sign of Pakistan-China friendship: FM Qureshi)

Snippet: Foreign Minister Shah Mahmood Qureshi has said that the One Belt and One Road Initiative (OBOR) will eventually become a sign of Pakistan-China friendship.

In a tweet, he said it will be an icon for bilateral friendship since both countries are charting new frontiers.

Body: 
He said the Great Wall of China is a symbol of China's triumphs and it has stood the test of time like the friendship between Pakistan and China.

He shared a picture of his autograph that says, "long live Pakistan-China friendship, the only iron brothers in the world."
