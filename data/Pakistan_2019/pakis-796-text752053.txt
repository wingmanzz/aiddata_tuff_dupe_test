Title: Chinese vice president leaves Lahore for China on completes 3 days visit to Pakistan

Snippet: Chinese vice president Wang Qishan has left Lahore for China on completion 3-day visit to Pakistan.

Chief Minister (CM) Punjab Usman Buzdar saw off distinguished guest at Lahore airport.

Body: 
He said Chinese vice president visit to Pakistan will further strengthen Sino-Pak friendship. The lasting and all weather friendship between the two countries can never come to end.

He held Pakistan and China are going side by side in the journey to development. During Prime Minister (PM) Imran Khan era these relations have touched new peaks. New chapters of affinity and cooperation are being authored between the two brothers.

Chinese vice president said he cannot forget the love showered on him during his visit to Lahore. China will continue to stand with Pakistan in the journey to development and prosperity. We will take friendship; to new heights by working on new dimensions of mutual cooperation.

Before his departure Chinese vice president visited Badshahi mosque museum. Chinese consulate general also accompanied Chinese vice president.
