Title: Chinese diplomat in Pakistan elevated to ambassadorial position

Snippet: Wang Yu, Consulate General of China in Karachi has been elevated to ambassadorial position, with the new assignment as his country's envoy to Afghanistan. During his tenure as Consulate General of China in Karachi from 2016 to 2019, the development of China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) saw tremendous progress.

According to Gwadar Pro Net, in the jurisdiction of China's Consulate in Karachi major projects were handled under CPEC, such as Gwadar Port and Gwadar Free Zone, besides the energy projects including Qasim Coal-fired Power Plant, Hub Coal-fired Power Plant, Dawo Wind Power Plant, and Thar Power Plant.

Body: 
Wang Yu played an active role in construction of Multan-Sukkur Mortorway, connecting the surrounding regions to the economic and cultural center of Southern Punjab Province.
