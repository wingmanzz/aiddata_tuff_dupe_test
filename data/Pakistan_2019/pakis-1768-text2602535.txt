Title: Adviser: (2nd phase of China-Pakistan FTA to be signed this month: Dawood)

Snippet: The second phase of China-Pakistan Free Trade Agreement will be signed in Beijing, during the forthcoming visit of Prime Minister Imran Khan to China this month.

This was stated by Advisor to Prime Minister on Commerce and Investment, Abdul Razzak Dawood while talking to China Economic Net at the Second International Textile Exhibition in Lahore.

Body: 
He said the best exporters from Pakistan will go with the Prime Minister to Beijing to connect with Chinese business people, inviting them to relocate to Pakistan.
