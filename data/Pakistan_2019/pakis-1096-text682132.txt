Title: Pakistan, Chinese Air forces begin joint exercise 'Shaheen-VIII

Snippet: The eighth annual joint exercise of Pakistan and Chinese Air forces, 'Shaheen-VIII', has started at northwest China. According to a report, the joint training aims to improve training standards of the two air forces through mutual learning. It will also help enhance close relations between the two countries in general and develop a mechanism for interoperability of both air forces in particular.
