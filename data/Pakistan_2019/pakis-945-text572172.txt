Title: Athletes off to China for training

Snippet: Five Pakistani athletes namely Mehboob Ali, Mohammad Naeem, Aziz-ur-Rehman and Samiullah departed to China late Monday night to undergo training ahead of this year's Tokyo Olympics.

South Asian Games gold medallist Arshad Nadeem, who has already qualified for the 2020 Games, will join the athletes on January 10.

Body: 
The latest training in China will help the Pakistani athletes to hone their skills for all international competitions coming ahead.
