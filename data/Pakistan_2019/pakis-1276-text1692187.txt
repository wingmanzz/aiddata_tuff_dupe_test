Title: Indigenously overhauled JF-17 aircraft rolled out

Snippet: Pakistan Aeronautical Complex (PAC) Kamra on Thursday achieved another milestone by rolling out first indigenously overhauled JF-17 fighting aircraft.

Pakistan Air force (PAF) held a ceremony in this regard at Aircraft Rebuild Factory (ARF) Kamra.

Body: 
Out of the first four JF- 17 aircraft, two were overhauled in China; whereas, the other two were indigenously overhauled in ARF Kamra.

Chief of the Air Staff, Air Chief Marshal Mujahid Anwar Khan was the Chief Guest at the ceremony.

Addressing on this occasion, the air chief said: 'We are living in technology-intensive world where self-reliance and indigenization are key to effectively addressing modern challenges.

'PAF has been relentlessly pursuing these goals and has achieved remarkable capability'.

He said JF-17 overhaul is indeed a significant achievement, which could not have been achieved without the dedication and hard work put in by the officers and technicians of Aircraft Rebuild Factory (ARF).

He also appreciated the continuous support of Chinese Aviation Industries including the top management of Aviation Industry Corporation of China (AVIC), China National Aero-technology Import and Export Corporation (CATIC), China Aviation Industry Corporation (CAIC) and CADI in our journey towards self-reliance.

PAC Chairman Air Marshal Ahmer Shehzad, CATIC President Yang Ying, AVIC Vice President Mr Wang Wel, CAIC President China Aviation Industry Corporation Zhan Jianping along with other senior civil and defense officials were also present on the occasion.
