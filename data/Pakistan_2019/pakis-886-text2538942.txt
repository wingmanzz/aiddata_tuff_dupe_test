Title: PM Khan greets Chinese people on 70th anniversary of People's Republic of China

Snippet: Prime Minister Imran Khan Tuesday greeted the Chinese people on the 70th anniversary of the Peoples' Republic of China and said Pakistan-China relations had grown from strength to strength and China Pakistan Economic Corridor project was reflection of our mutual commitment to this enduring relationship.

'Today on the 70th Anniversary of the People's Republic of China I want to extend our nation's greetings to our true and steadfast friend China. I have always admired China's development and fight against corruption,' the prime minister said in a series of tweets.

Body: 
Chinese citizens celebrated the 70th anniversary of the People's Republic of China during a parade in Beijing's Tiananmen Square on October 1.
