Title: Pakistan receives $2.2 bln from China

Snippet: The State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) has received US 2.2 billion from China, said a message received from the bank on Monday.

"The SBP has received RMB 15 billion value, equivalent to $2.2 billion as proceeds of the loan obtained by government of Pakistan from China," the message added.

Body: 
With this deposit, the country's forex exchange reserves have strengthened while the situation of balance of payment has also been improved.
