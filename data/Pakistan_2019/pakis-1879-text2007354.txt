Title: Chinese diplomat briefed on tourism, investment in other sectors

Snippet: Economic and Commercial Consul General of China Dr Wang Shenwa met with Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Senior Minister Muhammad Atif Khan and discussed with him matters for promotion of tourism and investment in various sectors.

Hassan Dawood Butt, Chief Executive Officer of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa's Board of Investment, was also present on the occasion, said a handout. Matters related to CPEC and Rashakai Economic Zones also came under discussion at the meeting.

Body: 
The senior minister said various projects were under progress for the development of tourism in the province and four integrated tourist zones were being built, including 14 new tourist destinations.

He said that over Rs1 billion have been earmarked to promote religious tourism. The Chinese consul general expressed interest in various projects for the development of tourism in the province and praised the plan to create tourism zones.

At the meeting, China Tourism Department and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Tourism Department agreed to benefit from each other's experiences and work together for the promotion of tourism.

The consul general expressed his interest in investing in other sectors, including integrated tourism zones, theme parks at Hund in Swabi, and assured to attend the conference on the promotion of religious tourism in March. 'Bloggers from China, including Buddhist religious leaders, will also attend the conference,' he added.
