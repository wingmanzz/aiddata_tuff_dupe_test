Title: Chinese Consul General calls on Mian Aslam Iqbal

Snippet: Chinese Consul General Mr Long Dingbin called on Provincial Minister for Industries and Trade Mian Aslam Iqbal at Punjab Investment Board and Trade office here on Friday. Bilateral economic cooperation, investment of Chinese companies and various matters of mutual interests came under discussion during the meeting. While talking on the occasion, the provincial minister said "Pak-China friendship is everlasting and converted into an economic co-operation." He said various Chinese companies had made investment in Punjab while other companies were also ready to invest here. He said that Punjab government had created an investment-friendly environment in the province and all possible facilities were being provided to investors. The Chinese Consul General said "CPEC is a great milestone of the friendship and we will enhance cooperation with Punjab." Chief Executive Officer Punjab Board of Investment and Trade, Jehanzeb Burhana was also present.
