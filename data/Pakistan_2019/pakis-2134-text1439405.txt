Title: Pakistan Air Force pilot killed in training jet crash

Snippet: Islamabad (dpa) - A Pakistan Air Force fighter jet crashed on Wednesday, killing the pilot, officials said.

The Chinese-made F-7PG aircraft was on a "routine operational training mission" when it crashed near Mastung in the south-western province of Balochistan, air force spokesman Waseem Ahmad Khan said.

Body: 
An inquiry has been ordered to determine the exact cause of the crash, the spokesman said.

Trainer aircraft used by the Pakistani air force, which procures most of its military hardware from China, are routinely involved in deadly mishaps.
