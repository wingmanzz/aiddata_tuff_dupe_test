Title: Some 1270 KM long highways to be constructed in second phase of CPEC

Snippet: One thousand two hundred seventy kilometer long highways will be constructed in second phase of China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC).

According to China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) Authority, removing barriers on the way to compeltion of corridor project is top prorities of the governmnet.

Body: 
The authority said it has been decided to increase speed up work on western-route of CPEC in second phase. The highways network will be constructed from Gilgit to Chitral and Dera Ismail Khan to Zhob.

The officials said the construction of roads network will not only create jobs opportunities for the countrymen but will strengthen the national economy. China can be taken an example as the country has brought millions of its citizens out of poverity and put the country on the path of development and progress.

The ruling governmnet was taking all possible measures to eleminate poverity from the country and was facilitating foreign investors to invest in different sectors in Pakistan. They hoped that the 'One Belt, One Road' project can change the destiny of this century.

The officials maintained that CPEC appeared helpful in overcoming power crisis in the country, adding that unintrupted power supply is important to attract foreign investors in the country.
