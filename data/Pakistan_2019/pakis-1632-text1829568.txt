Title: Chinese company of DHPP provides Ramadan package to 60 families of Kohistan

Snippet: Chinese Construction Company working at Dasu Hydro Power Project (DHPP) Kohistan on Tuesday distributed Ramadan package amongst 60 families in order to provide relief to the poor segment of the area during holy month of Ramadan.

DC Upper Kohistan Hamid ur Rehman, Assistant Commissioner Dasu Anwar Akbar Khan, Civil worker Abdul Jabbar Khan, representative of China company Mr. Than and Chomister Lue were present during the distribution ceremony where the poor families received the package.

Body: 
Earlier, during the 2nd Ramadan different Chinese contractor companies including CCECC, CGGC, Power China, Gamson, Fisthig and Missharo distributed 300 bags flour amongst the locals at government high school Upper Kohistan where a large number of the people were present.
