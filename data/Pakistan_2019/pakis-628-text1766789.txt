Title: Analysis: (PM's visit to China will help boost economy: Analysts)

Snippet: Analysts say Prime Minister Imran Khan's visit to China to attend second Belt and Road Forum will boost economy of Pakistan.

Talking in a Radio program on Thursday, former ambassador Fauzia Nasreen said Belt and Road initiative aims to strengthen infrastructure, trade, and investment among regional countries.

Body: 
Taking part in the program former ambassador Mushtaq Ahmed Mehar said it is the result of successful foreign policy of Pakistan that it is emerging as an economic hub.
