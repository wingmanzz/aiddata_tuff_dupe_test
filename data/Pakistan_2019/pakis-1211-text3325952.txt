Title: FPCCI signs MoU with CCIIP

Snippet: A five member FPCCI delegation led by Dr. Mirza Ikhiar Baig, Senior Vice President FPCCI, attended the Int'l Investment Forum organized by the China Council for International Investment Promotion (CCIIP) from September 7-10, 2019 at Xiamen, China.

Dr. Baig participated in the panel discussion at the investment forum attended by former Prime Minister of Egypt Prof. Dr. Essam Abdel Aziz Sharaf, former German Defense Minister Rudolf Albert Scharping, former Secretary Commerce US Carlos Gutierrez, deputy prime minister of Serbia Rasim Ljajic and president CCIIP Madame Ma Xiuhong on the uncertainties of world's economy such as the fate of Brexit, Sino-US trade war, falling oil prices and instability of international currencies. He said major economies must resolve trade war to avoid economic recession.

Body: 
Baig and the FPCCI delegation also attended the China-Pakistan Investment Cooperation seminar where he highlighted the investment opportunities in SEZs in Pakistan. An MoU was signed by Dr. Mirza Ikhtiar Baig, leader of the Pakistani delegation, and Liu Zuozhang, EVP and Sec. Gen of CCIIP, for establishing the China-Pakistan Joint Business and Investment Committee. It was witnessed by Consul General of Pakistan Dr. Diyar Khan, President CCIIP Madame Ma Xiuhong, Zubair Tufail, Anwar Qureshi, Malik Sohail Hussain and Mahmood Ahmed.
