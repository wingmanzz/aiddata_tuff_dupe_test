Title: Qishan's visit to inject new momentum to Sino-Pak ties

Snippet: Vice-President Wang Qishan's two-day visit to Pakistan, starting today will inject new momentum into further developing the two countries' all-weather strategic partnership, officials said here on Saturday.

The Chinese government attached high importance to the visit, wishing it opens new avenues of bilateral cooperation in meeting the common aspirations of their people. During visit, Wang will meet with President Dr. Arif Alvi, and hold talks with Prime Minister Imran Khan. Both sides will exchange views on bilateral relations and international and regional issues.

Body: 
According to the officials, Wang's visit to Pakistan will further deepen high-level exchanges between the two countries, consolidate their friendship and mutual trust. It will help promote the construction of the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor and pragmatic cooperation in various fields, and enhance people-to-people exchanges. China, believes that Wang Qishan's visit will further deepen China-Pakistan high-level exchanges, consolidate friendship and mutual trust, promote the construction of China-Pakistan economic corridor and pragmatic cooperation in various fields.
