Title: CPEC to further strengthen between Pakistan-China ties: Mian Iftikhar

Snippet: Central Secretary General of Awami National Party (ANP) and former Provincial Minister for Information, Mian Iftikhar Hussain Monday said the mega CPEC project would further strengthen ties between Pakistan and China.

These views were expressed during his visit to China Window set up in Peshawar for display of Chinese culture, said a press statement issued here.

Body: 
Mian Iftikhar said Pak-China friendship will remain matter of pride forever and the ongoing CPEC project will not only improve economic condition of Pakistan but further strengthen the already existing ties between the two neighboring countries.

He said ANP delegation held a meeting with Chinese Ambassador and told him to add western route in the CPEC.

Mian Iftikhar said Chinese authorities have given heed to this proposal while realizing importance of KP and merged tribal districts.

ANP leader said Chinese gained independence after Pakistan and started its economic improvement in 1978 and within 40 years of hard work and dynamic leadership, the country has emerged as super power among comity of the nations.

He also termed welcoming establishment of China Window in Peshawar and expressed the hope that the center will apprise people of the area about Chinese culture besides promotion of KP tourism by informing visiting Chinese delegations about scenic beauty of KP.

Later, Mian Iftikhar visited different galleries at China Window and expressed keen interest in pictures, books and paintings displayed there.
