Title: Rs 100 mln allocated for BOI in PSDP 2019-20

Snippet: The government has allocated Rs 100 million in Public Sector Development Programme (PSDP) 2019-20 for one on- going scheme of the Board of Investment (BOI) for the industrial promotion and Investment in the country.

According to the details, the government has allocated Rs 100 million for establishment of Project Management Unit (PMU) on China Pakistan Economic Corridor - Industrial Cooperation Development Project (CPEC-ICDP) in PSDP 2019-20 for industrial cooperation between Pakistan and China.

Body: 
The total estimated cost of the Project Management Unit (PMU) of China Pakistan Economic Corridor - Industrial Cooperation Development Project (CPEC-ICDP) is Rs 339.281 million, it added.
