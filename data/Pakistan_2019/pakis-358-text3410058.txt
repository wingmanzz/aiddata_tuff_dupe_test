Title: ABL, Bank of China-Pakistan ink MoU

Snippet: Allied Bank Limited (ABL) and Bank of China-Pakistan (BOCP) have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) for developing business synergies and formalizing a strategic partnership which aims to promote trade and investments relating to China's One Belt One Road Initiative.

The MoU was signed by ABL's CEO, Tahir Hassan Qureshi and Country Head and CEO of BOCP Liu Xiaofei in a ceremony held at Bank of China Headquarters in Beijing in May. Tahir Hassan Qureshi congratulated the management of BOC and extended ABL's cooperation on various fronts. Under this MoU, both banks have agreed to collaborate with each other on RMB Business, trade finance, treasury and corporate banking transactions. The agreement also encourages joint syndication and project financing of various projects in Pakistan.
