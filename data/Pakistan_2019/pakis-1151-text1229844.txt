Title: 'Pak has benefited from China's socio-economic measures'

Snippet: Pakistan's government will get benefit from the Chinese socio-economic development measures to remove poverty by empowering Pakistan's agricultural and industrial sector, said a federal minister. Minister of planning, development, and reform Makhdum Khusro Bakhtiar made the remarks on the east Punjab province.
