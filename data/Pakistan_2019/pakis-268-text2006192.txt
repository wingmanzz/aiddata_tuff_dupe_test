Title: Minister: (Pakistan to benefit from China's expertise in Railways Sector: Rashid)

Snippet: Railways Minister Sheikh Rashid Ahmad has said that Pakistan will benefit from China's expertise in Railways Sector.

Talking to newsmen in Lahore on Monday, he said that Main Line One project is the jugular vein of China Pakistan Economic Corridor project.

Body: 
He said that investigations should be conducted into foreign funding case against all political parties.
