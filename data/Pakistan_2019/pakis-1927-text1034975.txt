Title: Fawad: (China always provided support, assistance to Pak's economic, social development: Fawad)

Snippet: Federal Minister for Science and Technology Chaudhry Fawad Hussain has said that China has always provided support and assistance to Pakistan's economic and social development.

He was addressing the inaugural ceremony of 7th Pak-China Business Forum and Industrial Expo in Lahore on Monday.

Body: 
The Minister said that China Pakistan Economic Corridor Project has transformed the ties between two countries into economic cooperation.

He said that there are vast opportunities of investment in solar power sector in the country.

He invited Chinese investors to establish manufacturing plants of solar power products.
