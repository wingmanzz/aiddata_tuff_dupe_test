Title: RIM PETROCHEMICALS NO.2932 PET and RAW MATERIALS Purified Terephthalic Acid / Monoethylene Glycol

Snippet: Prices for purified terephthalic acid (PTA) on a bonded warehouse basis were in the range of $635-645/mt on Wednesday, down $40/mt from the previous day. China domestic prices dropped by Yuan 105/mt to Yuan 5,085-5,115/mt. The China domestic market remained under pressure from a continued decline in PTA futures on the Zhengzhou Commodity Exchange. Spot cargoes were discussed at Yuan 5,085-5,115/mt. The bonded warehouse prices faced a correction phase amid high price concerns in comparison with China domestic prices. Talks were heard at $635-645/mt.

Body: 
Prices for ethylene glycol (EG) on a CFR China basis retreated to $560-565/mt on Wednesday, down $8/mt from the previous day. China domestic prices eased by Yuan 25/mt to Yuan $4,810-4,880/mt. Both CFR China and China domestic prices were dragged down by a dip in overnight crude oil prices, coupled with looming concerns over a supply glut in the domestic market. Ample supply sentiment was boosted by recovering run rates at methanol-to-EG units in China. CFR China cargoes were heard talked at $560-565/mt while discussions took place at Yuan 4,810-4,880/mt in the China domestic market.
