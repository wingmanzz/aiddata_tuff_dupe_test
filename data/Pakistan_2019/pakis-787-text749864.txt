Title: Oracle Power Agrees Terms of Joint Venture for Pakistan Coal Project

Snippet: By Anthony O. Goriainoff

Oracle Power PLC (ORCP.LN) said Monday that it has reached an agreement with shareholder Sheikh Ahmed Dalmook Juma Al Maktoum and China National Coal Development Co Ltd.--a subsidiary of China National Coal Group Corp. (601898.SH)--regarding the joint-development of the Thar Block VI combined lignite coal mine and mine-mouth power plant in Pakistan.

Body: 
The U.K. energy company said that under the joint-venture agreement a special purpose vehicle will be created to develop the project, which will be funded on a 75% debt and 25% equity basis.

China National Coal Development Co will have a 73% stake of the joint venture, with 15% owned by the private office of Mr. Al Maktoum and 13% held by Oracle.

"Our new partners share Oracle's vision and can see the truly transformational impact that the development of Thar Block VI can provide," Oracle Chief Executive Officer Naheed Memon said.

Shares at 0840 were up 0.02 pence, or 1.9%, at 1.33 pence.
