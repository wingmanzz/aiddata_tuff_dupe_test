Title: China offers to provide clean drinking water to 1500 schools

Snippet: China Monday offered to provide clean drinking water facility to 1500 schools in various parts of Pakistan.

China Deputy Ambassador extended the offer during a meeting with federal minister of Science and Technology Chaudhry Fawad Hussain who called on him along with the delegation of Chinese Academy of Science, said a press release.

Body: 
The minister asked China for extending assistance to bring Pakistani laboratories at par with the international standard.

The deputy envy also said that China will also provide information technology assistance to Media University. He also stressed the need for sending Pakistani citizen to China for taking part in various training programmes.
