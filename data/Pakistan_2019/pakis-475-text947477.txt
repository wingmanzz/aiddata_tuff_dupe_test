Title: Chinese Companies Win EPC Contract for Pakistan Refining & Chemical Project

Snippet: China Energy Engineering Group Guangdong Electric Power Design Institute, China Gezhouba Group International Engineering Co., Ltd. have signed EPC contract with FALCONOILS (PVT) LTD on the Petroleum Refining & Chemical project in Khyber-Pashtun Province, Pakistan.

The project includes a petroleum refining & chemical plant, with capacities of 100 bl/day or 5 000 kt/a, 100MW captive power station, 277 km of affiliated oil pipelines and oil storage tanks with capacities of 4.40 million cubic meters.
