Title: Minister: (PTI Govt to bring country out of economic crisis within three years: Sh. Rashid)

Snippet: Minister for Railways Sheikh Rashid Ahmad has said that the PTI government will bring the country out of economic crisis within three years.

Talking to newsmen in Lahore on Saturday, he said that Main Line One project is the backbone of China-Pakistan Economic Corridor. The most prominent Chinese personality will be invited to inaugurate this project.

Body: 
He said today Muslims in India are staging protests against the policies of Modi's government. Earlier, the Railways Minister presided over a high level meeting at Railway Headquarters in Lahore.

The meeting was attended by Chairman Railways Board Habibur Rehman Gilani, CEO Railways Aijaz Ahmad Buriro and other high officials of the railways.
