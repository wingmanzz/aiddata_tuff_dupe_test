Title: Hafeez assures envoy of facilitating Chinese investors

Snippet: Advisor to Prime Minister on Finance Dr Abdual Hafeez Sheikh has assured Chinese ambassador that the government would facilitate Chinese investors by providing them all possible support to invest in Pakistan.

Ambassador of People's Republic of China Yao Jing on Monday called on Hafeez Sheikh and discussed matters of bilateral interest. He underscored the need for enhanced collaboration between the two countries in the areas of mutual benefit.

Body: 
The advisor appreciated China's role in the social and economic development of Pakistan and stated that Pakistan highly values its ties with China, which is a sincere friend and has always supported Pakistan in difficult times.

Sheikh added that environment in Pakistan for foreign investment is conducive and Chinese businessmen should benefit from it to make investment in various sectors. He conveyed that the government would facilitate Chinese investors by providing them all possible support to invest in Pakistan.

Yao Jing said that the Chinese investors want to invest in Pakistan and their confidence over the policies and leadership of Pakistan is getting increased. He reiterated his government's resolve to continue its support for early execution of various projects under the CPEC. The ambassador also congratulated Dr Abdul Hafeez Sheikh on assuming the portfolio of adviser.

The meeting discussed the forthcoming visit of the Prime Minister of Pakistan to China and expressed the hope that the visit would further strengthen the existing multifaceted relations between the two neighbours.
