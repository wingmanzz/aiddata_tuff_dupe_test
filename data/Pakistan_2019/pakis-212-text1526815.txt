Title: Minister: (ML-1 project will provide around 150,000 job opportunities: Sh Rasheed)

Snippet: Railways Minister Sheikh Rasheed Ahmad has said that Main Line One project will provide around one hundred and fifty thousand job opportunities.

Addressing a press conference in Lahore on Saturday, he said Pakistan and China will sign a preliminary agreement for the design of the ML-1 project during the upcoming visit of Prime Minister Imran Khan to China.

Body: 
He said that after completion of ML-1 project, the travel duration from Lahore to Karachi will be reduced to eight hours.
