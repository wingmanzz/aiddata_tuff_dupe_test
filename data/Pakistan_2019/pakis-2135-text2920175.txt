Title: FM Qureshi to reach China today

Snippet: Foreign Minister Shah Mehmood Qureshi will reach China on Wednesday (today) after completing his three-day official visit to Japan.

According to reports, FM Qureshi is visiting China on invitation of his Chinese counterpart. Qureshi will hold a meeting with top Chinese officials tomorrow.

Body: 
Both the leaders of Pak-China will discuss bilateral ties and the overall regional situation.

Earlier, Qureshi on Tuesday met with his Japanese counterpart, Taro Kono in Tokyo and exchanged views on bilateral relations, promotion of cooperation in various fields and resolved to continue joint efforts for peace and stability in the region.

During the meeting, Shah Mehmood Qureshi briefed his Japanese counterpart on regional situation and thanked Taro Kono for inviting him in his country.

Pakistan and Japan also agreed to further bolster their bilateral ties on leadership as well as masses level.

It may be noted here that Prime Minister Imran Khan will depart to Beijing on April 27 and he would be accompanied by cabinet members.
