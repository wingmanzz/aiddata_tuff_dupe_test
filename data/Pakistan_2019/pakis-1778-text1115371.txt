Title: PM policy for steering poor segments of society from mire of poverty to help revive national economy

Snippet: Policies being pursued by Prime Minister (PM) Imran Khan for steering the poor and marginalized sections of society from the mire of poverty will help revive national economy and the economic activities to be generated by the projects under CPEC will upgrade the standard of living of the people. Planning Commission (PC) authorities said Pakistan and China have vast potential to bolster cooperation in poverty alleviation program . They said Pakistan will pursue Chinese model to extricate its citizens from the mire of poverty. Building fundamental infrastructure can play vital role in poverty alleviation campaign.
