Title: Chairman PFMA hails Govt decision on signing of FTA II

Snippet: The Chairman of Pakistan Footwear Manufacturer Association (PFMA), Hassan Javed Thursday applauded the government decision of signing the phase II of Pak-China Free Trade Agreement (FTA).

He said that this step will help in promoting the exports of Footwear through zero custom duty on some of the raw material used for footwear manufacturing process.

Body: 
He added that this initiative will not only boost exports of footwear but will also create job and investment opportunity in this sector.

He also commended the efforts of Ministry of Commerce and Industries for taking the exporting industries on board in the preparation process of the final draft of FTA Phase II.

He said that the successful implementation of Phase I of Pak-China FTA had encouraging impacts on bilateral trade and the decision to take the initiative to another level by signing its phase II will further boost the bilateral trade between the countries.
