Title: Chinese invited to invest in special economic zones

Snippet: Provincial Minister for Industries and Trade Mian Aslam Iqbal has said that solid steps has been taken for ease of doing business and special economic zones are being setup on thousands acres of land. Chinese investors can invest in these special economic zone, we will provide them all necessary facilities.

He visited Chinese Consulate in Lahore on Saturday and met Consul General Long Dingbin.
