Title: PM Imran to take ministers into confidence on China visit

Snippet: ISLAMABAD: After completing a successful visit to China, Pakistan Prime Minister Imran Khan will chair multiple sessions today (Thursday) in Islamabad and take his ministers into confidence regarding the tour. According to details, a meeting will also be held to deliberate on the premier's upcoming visit to Saudi Arabia on October 13 and Iran later this month.

PM Imran is also expected to visit Saudi Arabia again on October 29 to participate in three-day Future Investment Initiative Conference (FIIC) to be held in Riyadh. Saudi King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud has invited PM Imran to attend Future Investment Initiative Conference to be held in Saudi capital from October 29 till October 31. Saudi government has also invited Indian prime minister Narendra Modi in the upcoming event.

Body: 
Earlier today, top officials including Chinese Deputy Foreign Minister bid farewell and presented guard of honor to Prime Minister Imran Khan while he was departing for Pakistan. PM Imran Khan reached Pakistan along with his delegation after completion of a successful three-day visit, strengthening bilateral ties between both countries.
