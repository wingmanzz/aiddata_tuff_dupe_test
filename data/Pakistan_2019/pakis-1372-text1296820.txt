Title: Delegation from Guiyang Fangzhou Hi-Tech Company Limited visited PBIT - Press Note issued by Punjab Board of Investment and Trade

Snippet: July 09, 2019 (PPI-OT)

Following is the text of press note issued by Punjab Board of Investment and Trade (PBIT)

Body: 
Quote

A delegation from Guiyang Fangzhou Hi-Tech Co. Ltd. visited PBIT to explore investment opportunity in manufacturing of CCTV cameras. The Director of China Desk PBIT shared the investment and Special Economic Zone (SEZ) regime and assured full of support and facilitation.
