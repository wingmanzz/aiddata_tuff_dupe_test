Title: Asst Prof Islamia University participates in seminar in China

Snippet: Dr Muhammad Naveed Aslam Malghani, Assistant Prof, Plant Pathology, University College of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences, the Islamia University of Bahawalpur participated in a seminar organized by Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs in China.

The scientists from seven countries under Belt and Road initiatives participated in the seminar. He was invited as a guest of honour at the closing ceremony held in Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, Beijing where he was awarded coordinator of the seminar.
