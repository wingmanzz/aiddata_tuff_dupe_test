Title: PM breaks ground for prefab homes plant

Snippet: Prime Minister Imran Khan on Wednesday said that his government would welcome foreign investment with modern technology to bring improvement in the lives of common man.

Addressing at the groundbreaking ceremony of a plant for Easy Prefabricated Homes by a leading Chinese company Henan D.R. Construction Group in Faisalabad, held at PM House here, he said that prefab construction was the best option for low-cost and quicker housing set-up.
