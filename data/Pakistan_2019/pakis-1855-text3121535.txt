Title: Dipl: (China to continue to play constructive role for regional stability: Envoy)

Snippet: Pakistan's Ambassador to China Masood Khalid has expressed hope that Beijing will continue to play a constructive and positive role in stabilizing the situation in South Asia.

In an interview with Phoenix TV, he said Pakistan is providing full security to Chinese nationals working on various projects in the country.

Body: 
Meanwhile in a separate interview, he said over the past five years, eleven projects have been completed under the China Pakistan Economic Corridor Project while eleven more projects are in the construction stage.

He said the total investment of these twenty two projects is about 18.9 billion dollars, while twenty more projects are in the pipeline.
