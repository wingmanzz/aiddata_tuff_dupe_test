Title: Minister: (Kashmir issue will come under discussion during PM's China visit: Rashid)

Snippet: Minister for Railways Sheikh Rashid Ahmad has said that economy and Kashmir issue will come under discussion during Prime Minister Imran Khan visit to China.

In his special message before leaving for China last night, he said Pak-China friendship is higher than Himalayas and China is all seasoned friend of Pakistan.

Body: 
He urged the opposition not to put Pakistan in crises to gain their political interests. He said Imran Khan will play a historic role in freedom of Kashmir and pulling country out of crisis.
