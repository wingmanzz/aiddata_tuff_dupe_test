Title: President confers Pakistan's highest civil award on Chinese vice president

Snippet: President Dr Arif Alvi Sunday conferred Nishan-e-Pakistan, the highest civil award of the country, on Chinese Vice President Wang Qishan to recognize his services in promoting Pak-China ties.

The award was conferred at a special investiture ceremony held here at the President House that was attended by chairman Senate, services chiefs, members of the federal cabinet, parliamentarians and diplomats.

Body: 
Wang Qishan arrived here earlier in the day on a three-day visit to interact with the prime minister and president besides signing agreements between the two countries to further enhance bilateral ties.

Wang Qishan is a great friend of Pakistan and ardent supporter of Pak-China Friendship who had played a pivotal role in spearheading the revolutionary agenda of President Xi Jinping.
