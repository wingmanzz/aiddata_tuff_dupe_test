Title: Sehat Insaf Cards will be provided to over 15 million people in two years: Dr. Firdous

Snippet: Special Assistant to the Prime Minister on Information and Broadcasting Dr. Firdous Ashiq Awan says Sehat Insaf Cards' facility will be extended to over fifteen million people across the country in next two years.

Addressing a distribution ceremony of health cards among deserving people in Sialkot on Sunday, she said the government has also started health reforms program in Punjab to facilitate the common people.

Body: 
Dr Firdous Ashiq Awan said nearly one hundred sixty five thousand health cards will be distributed in district Sialkot.

She said holders of the health cards will get the facility of free medical treatment for cancer, liver and heart diseases as well as all major surgeries at designated hospitals.

On the occasion, she also announced to launch various initiatives of Prime Minister Imran Khan, which include easy loans for backyard poultry, cattle farming, and procurement of fertilizers and seeds on subsidized rates for farmers of district Sialkot.

Meanwhile Firdous Ashiq Awan says Prime Minister Imran Khan is visiting China from today (Monday) to discuss bilateral economic and trade relations and CPEC related projects with Chinese leadership.

Addressing a news conference in Lahore, she said the situation in occupied Kashmir and India's aggression along the Line of Control will also come under discussion during talks between Pakistani and Chinese leadership.

The Special Assistant said Pakistan's economy is moving forward on the positive trajectory and deficit is witnessing a decline.
