Title: Pakistan to follow Chinese economic model to get rid of poverty: Sarwar

Snippet: Delegation headed by Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Yao Jang met with Governor Punjab Ch. Muhammad Sarwar. They discussed Pak China relationship and strategic as well as economic importance of CPEC.

Governor said that Pak china friendship is higher than mountains and deeper than ocean. Pakistan will ensure taking every possible step to get advantage of the Chinese economic model which has made unprecedented progress by lifting more than 700 million people out of the poverty line in last three decades.

Body: 
Those who conspire against the friendship of Pakistan and China are Pakistan's enemies and they cannot see Pakistan making progress.

On Thursday, A delegation including Chinese Consul General Lahore Long ding bin headed by Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Yao Jang met Governor Punjab Ch. Muhammad Sarwar where issues of mutual interest, Chinese investment in Pakistan and co-operation in different sectors including energy came into discussion. While interacting with the delegation, Governor Punjab Ch. Muhammad Sarwar said that Pak China friendship is strengthening with each passing day and has been flourishing for the decades, CPEC is its vivid manifestation.

Pakistan is striving hard to create market confidence for the Chinese investors so that they may invest in Pakistan. Apart from that, Pakistan is also willing to enhance prospects of CPEC to alleviate poverty, educate masses and to increase energy production.
