Title: Contract signed for electro-mechanical works of Dasu Hydropower Project

Snippet: Water and Power Development Authority (WAPDA) has signed a contract worth 52.5 billion rupees with a joint venture of GE Hydro China and Power China Zhongnan Engineering Corporation for the construction of the Dasu Hydropower Project's Stage-I.

Addressing the ceremony, Water Resources Federal Secretary Muhammad Ashraf said the Ministry is committed to optimal utilization of hydropower resources for generating low-cost and environment-friendly electricity to meet the energy requirements.

Body: 
The contract includes design, supply and installation of six francis turbines, generators, main transformers, generator and station service switchgear along with related equipment.
