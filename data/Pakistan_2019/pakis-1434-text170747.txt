Title: Chinese Commercial Counsellor General meets KPK Senior Minister Atif Khan

Snippet: Economic and Commercial Counsellor General of China Dr. Wang Shenwa met with Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Senior Minister Atif Khan at his office and made a detailed discussion regarding promotion of tourism and investment in various sectors. Hassan Dawood Butt, CEO of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa's Board of Investment, was also present on the occasion. Detailed discussions regarding investment in various sectors including tourism, minerals, hydropower and other sectors were discussed on the occasion. There was also a detailed discussion on CPEC and Rashkai Economic Zones in the meeting.

Body: 
Chinese Commercial Counsellor General Congratulated Senior Minister Atif khan for Declaring the Best tourism Country for 2020. Apprising Chinese counsellor General regarding tourism Senior Minister said that various projects were under progress for development of tourism in the province, 4 integrated tourist zones were being constructed in the province, including 14 new tourist destinations. He said that more than one billion rupees have been earmarked to promote religious tourism. Counsellor General of China expressed his keen interest in various projects for the development of tourism in the province and said that the plan to create tourism zones was praiseworthy. Counsellor General praised provincial government's initiatives for religious tourism.
