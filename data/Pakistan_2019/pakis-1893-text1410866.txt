Title: Sindhi girl marries Chinese teacher

Snippet: Dr Falk Naz Miano, daughter of a retired professor Noor Muhammad Miano of Agricultural University Tando Jam, who had gone to a Chinese university three years ago on scholarship for her PhD, has married her Chinese teacher Dr Xinchao.

The Nikah ceremony was held at a local hotel where the other events of the marriage were performed according to the local Sindhi culture. The groom, Dr Xinchao, is also accompanied with his parents.

Body: 
Dr Falak Naz is the first Sindhi girl to marry a Chinese. She did her Masters in crop production from Agricultural University Jamshoro and later got enrolled at a Chinese university for PhD. The couple would return to China after staying for a week in Hyderabad.
