Title: MCC International offers support for improving PSM capacity

Snippet: The Chinese MCC International Incorporation Limited Thursday offered support for revival, Operationalization and improving the capacity of Pakistan Steel Mills on public-private partnership basis.

The offer was made during a call on meeting of Chinese delegation of MCC International Incorporation Limited, led by its Vice President, Wang Zhou with Adviser to Prime Minister on Finance, Revenue and Economic Affairs, Dr. Abdul Hafeez Shaikh here.

Body: 
The delegation showed interest to invest in the steel industry of Pakistan, said a press statement issued by the Finance Ministry.

On the occasion, the Adviser briefed the delegation about the scope of investment opportunities in Pakistan.

He informed the delegation that the government had taken a number of steps for improving the ease of business in the country.
