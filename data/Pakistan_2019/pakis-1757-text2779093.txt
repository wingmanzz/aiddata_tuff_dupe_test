ATH--SALAZAR-DOPING

DOHA, Qatar _ Renowned track coach Alberto Salazar has been kicked out of the world championships after being handed a four-year ban in a case long pursued by the U.S. Anti-Doping Agency. Salazar used to train four-time Olympic champion Mo Farah, who says he has "no tolerance for anyone who breaks the rules." The USADA ban went into effect Monday and the IAAF revoked Salazar's credential for the final six days in Doha. Salazar will appeal the ban. By Eddie Pells. SENT: 745 words, photos.
