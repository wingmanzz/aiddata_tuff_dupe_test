Title: CPEC long-term vision for development, connectivity: Chinese Envoy

Snippet: Job Programme Chinese Ambassador Yao Jing has emphasized the need to further expand people to people contacts between Pakistan and China. Addressing a function in connection with Chinese New Year celebrations here on Monday, he said China Pakistan Economic Corridor is a long-term vision for development, prosperity and connectivity. He expressed confidence that CPEC will explore new dimensions of cooperation and ties. Yao Jing said we believe that Pak-China friendship is getting stronger over the years. The Chinese Ambassador said Pakistani students will be granted more scholarships and internships in China.
