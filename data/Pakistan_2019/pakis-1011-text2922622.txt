Title: CPEC will boost Balochistan province: Zia Langove

Snippet: Asianet Pakistan Private Limited

QUETTA: Provincial Home Minister Mir Zia Langove has said China Pakistan Economic Corridor project will bring economic progress in Balochistan and Youth of Balochistan will get better opportunities of employment through CPEC. In a statement, the Home Minister Balochistan Mir Zia Langove with the advent of CPEC project industrial zones of Balochistan will also flourish.

Body: 
Balochistan has central position in CPEC project and under China Pakistan Economic Corridor project work is in progress on huge number of mega projects in Balochistan, he added. He said that Gwadar is also a important component of One Road One Belt project.

Under CPEC various projects including Gwadar Deep sea port, Gwadar international airport, Gwadar free zone, Pakistan and China Friendship Hospital and drinking water projects are underway. CPEC is among world best projects of the world and with the completion of the project CPEC will emerges as economic hub for the country and Balochistan.

Regional and international Enemies of Pakistan are against the mega project of CPEC that is why they have been spreading false propaganda of terrorism and extremism. He said entire Pakistan nation will not tolerate any conspiracy against CPEC.
