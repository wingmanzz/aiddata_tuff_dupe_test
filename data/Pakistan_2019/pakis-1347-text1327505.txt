Title: Provision of First Aid Can Help overcome health and Safety Risk in Schools

Snippet: First aid is a necessary skill that must be taught at schools to overcome the safety and health risk in educational institutes, said PRC Sindh Provincial Secretary Kanwar Waseem. He was addressing the representatives of schools at a ceremony for first aid boxes distribution among 99 schools of Karachi held on Friday. PRC Sindh in collaboration with International Committee of Red Cross (ICRC) is working to achieve the goal of 'One first aider in every home'.

Body: 
In this regard, PRC Sindh has provided first aid training in more than 220 schools of Sindh. In order to take ensure safety and effectiveness of emergency response, PRC Sindh has provided complimentary First aid boxes among the 99 schools of Karachi where the training has been conducted. A total of 33 schools from three districts of Karachi received the box in order to aid the emergency response at the educational institute. PRC Sindh has earlier distributed these boxes in Hyderabad and Jamshoro and will further conduct the distribution in Sukkur and Jacobabad in the next month with 33 boxes in schools of each district.
