Title: Chinese vice president arrives in Lahore

Snippet: China Vice President Wang Qishan arrived in the provincial capital on Monday for a two-day visit.

Punjab Chief Minister Sardar Usman Buzdar, provincial ministers and officials warmly welcomed the distinguished guest at the airport.

Body: 
During his two-day visit to the Punjab capital, the Chinese vice president along with his delegation, would attend a function at Haier factory on Raiwind Road and later attend a ceremony at a local hotel.

Wang Qishan will also visit Lahore Fort on Tuesday.
