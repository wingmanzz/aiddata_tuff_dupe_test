Title: CJP calls PRC meeting on Sep 26

Snippet: Chief Justice of Pakistan (CJP) Asif Saeed Khosa has convened Police Reform Committee (PRC) meeting on September 26.

CJP Asif Saeed Khosa has called the meeting of PRC in Islamabad. Chief Justice Lahore High Court will also attend this meeting. IGs' Police from all the four provinces will present their reports on performance of police in the meeting.

Body: 
CJP has also sought details of complaints against police from January 01 to September.
