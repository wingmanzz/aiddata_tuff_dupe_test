Title: Dipl: (CPEC project to develop infrastructure in Pakistan: Envoy)

Snippet: Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Yao Jing has said that China-Pakistan Economic Corridor will not only connect China with Gwadar but it would also lead to Kandahar, Afghanistan, Central Asia and Russia.

Speaking at a dialogue on CPEC in Karachi, he said that the project would develop infrastructure in Pakistan.

Body: 
He said that China is doing huge investment on a partnership basis and helping Pakistan in various sectors including education, technical education, agriculture and technology transfer.
