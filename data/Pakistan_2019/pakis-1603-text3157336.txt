Title: PTI delegation leaves for China on invitation of Chinese Communist Party

Snippet: A twenty four member PTI delegation has left for China on the invitation of Chinese Communist Party.

Before departure, the delegation also held a meeting with the PTI's Secretary General Amir Mahmood Kiani.

Body: 
In his remarks, the PTI's Secretary General said China is Pakistan's reliable and trustworthy friend. He said the cooperation between Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaaf and Chinese Communist Party is moving forward. He said the Chinese Communist Party is an excellent example of modern Democratic Party.

The PTI's Secretary General asked the party delegation to accrue maximum advantage from the experiences of the communist party.

During stay in China, the PTI delegation will be apprised about Chinese experiences and successes in the fields of economy, politics and administrative matters.
