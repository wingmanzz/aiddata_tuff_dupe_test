Title: Minister: (Railways infrastructure being improved under ML-1 project: Minister)

Snippet: Minister for Railways Sheikh Rashid Ahmad has said that the Railways infrastructure will be upgraded under the ML-1 project.

Talking to media persons in Rawalpindi on Monday, he said this project will significantly reduce the travel time. He said PTI government will complete its five years term.

Body: 
Earlier, addressing a ceremony in Rawalpindi on Monday, Sheikh Rashid Ahmed said that a Railway University will be established in cooperation with China.

He said land for the university will be provided by the Railways which will be constructed with the investment of China. The Minister said an IT University will also be established in Rawalpindi.
