Title: Chinese firm expresses readiness to investment in gas exploration sector

Snippet: A leading company of China on Wednesday expressed its readiness to invest in natural gas exploration and production sector of Pakistan.

The willingness came during a meeting between the delegation of Sichuan Chuanzhong Gas Investment Company Limited and Minister for Petroleum and Natural Resources Ghulam Sarwar Khan here.

Body: 
After financing gas exploration sector in China and India, now the company is interested to invest in tapping hydrocarbon resources in Pakistan, a Petroleum Division press release said.

The minister assured the delegation that the government would provide conducive envirionment to foreign investors.

The delegation thanked the minister for extending cooperation.
