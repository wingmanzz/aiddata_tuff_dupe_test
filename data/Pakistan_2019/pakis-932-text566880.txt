Title: Pakistan China friendship sweeter than honey: Nazim

Snippet: District Nazim Mohammad Asim Khan said has said that Pakistan and China enjoy deep trust and the friendly relations between the two countries are deeply rooted in the people.

'Pakistan China friendship is higher than mountains, deeper than the ocean and sweeter than honey,' the Nazim observed during an inauguration ceremony held to mark start of Chinese Language Classes in the China Windo here on Wednesday.

Body: 
He said that establishment of China Windo would strengthen bilateral relations and people-to-people contact between the two friendly countries.

He said Chinese ambassador had great love for Peshawar and people of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, wishing the CPEC's blessings trickled down to the people of the province.

He said that center would also help open news avenues for the socio-economic development of two countries and suggested for the center's management to early measures for resolving problems of people belonging to trade and commerce industry to further strengthen economic relations between the two countries.

He also appreciated renovation and decoration art of the center, saying it would help peoples of the two countries to understand each other's culture.

He also assured support of the district administration to the Management and extended invitation to the Chinese ambassador for visit to Peshawar, adding traditional reception would be hosted in his honor.

He also appreciated the ambassador for playing his part to launch the China Windo.
