Title: Qureshi, Chinese Vice President discuss bilateral relations including progress CPEC project

Snippet: Foreign Minister Makhdoom Shah Mahmood Qureshi called on Chinese Vice President Wang Qishan in Beijing on Tuesday and discussed bilateral relations including progress on China Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) project.

Shah Mahmood Qureshi said Pakistan desires cordial relations with all the neighboring countries. He said Pakistan and China enjoy exemplary relations. He said Pakistan desires a strong economic partnership with China.

Body: 
The foreign minister said that Pakistan will also benefit from the Chinese experience in poverty alleviation and eradication of corruption.
