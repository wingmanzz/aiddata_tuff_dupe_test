Title: GB: (Function in connection with Chinese National Day held in Gilgit)

Snippet: A function in connection with Chinese National Day was held in Gilgit. It was organized by Chines overseas foundation, Gilgit-Baltistan. Speaking on the occasion Minister works Dr Muhammad Iqbal congratulated Chinese for their national day celebrations.

He said Pakistan and China are time tasted friends enjoining cordial relations. Deputy Speaker Gilgit-Baltistan Assembly Jafarullah Khan said CPEC project will further strengthen ties between both friend countries.

Body: 
He paid tributes to Chines Leader Mao who put china on path of development and prosperity. Later, they cut birth anniversary cake.
