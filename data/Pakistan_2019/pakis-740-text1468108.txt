Title: Partnership investment with Chinese companies discussed

Snippet: QUETTA: The Chief Executive Officer of China Merchants Group (CMG), Mr. Tau called on the Chief Minister Balochistan, Jam Kamal Khan here on Thursday.

Chief Secretary Balochistan Captain (Retd) Fazeel Asghar was also present on the occasion. During the course of meeting, the matters pertaining to promotion of cooperation, partnership and investment with the Chinese company in various sectors came up for threadbare discussion.

Body: 
The Chief Minister also talked with the CEO China Merchants Group about declaring the Korla city of China and Quetta as sister cities. It may be mentioned here that Korla is the second largest city in Xinjiang.

They also discussed the matters about the academic scholarships for students of Balochistan and assigning the chairmanship of Pakistan-China center to the Chief Minister Balochistan. Similarly, they also considered the matters regarding investment in the minerals sector of Balochistan besides provision of an assistance package for the province.

The Chief Minister hailed the provision of assistance of the China Merchants Group in various sectors of the province. He also welcomed the investments to be made by the Chinese Group in Balochistan.

He assured the wholehearted cooperation to the Group in this regard on behalf of the provincial government. Earlier, needless to mention here is, the Chief Minister shed light on the great potential of the province in different sectors mainly the mineral sector and natural resources.
