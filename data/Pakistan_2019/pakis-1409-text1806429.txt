Title: 250 million investment made in Gawadar

Snippet: Investment of 250 million has been made in Gawadar port under China Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC).

5 modern cranes have been installed. 2 lac 20 thousand gallons clean drinking water is being provided daily. Equipments for sewerage disposal system and cargo handling have been installed.

Body: 
According to CPEC Secretariat, Gawadar port between Pakistan and China will help to make it international and modern port. By giving practical shape to economic zones way will be paved for industrial revolution in Pakistan.

18.9 KM long 4-lane highway is being built under Gawadar East bay expressway project and this highway will link Gawadar port to Makran coastal highway, free zone and Containers terminals.
