Title: Minister: (Visit of Vice President of China demonstrates strong bilateral ties: Qureshi)

Snippet: Foreign Minister Shah Mehmood Qureshi has said that five Memorandums of Understanding will be signed during the visit of Chinese Vice President.

Talking to media, he said the visiting dignitary will also inaugurate some of the projects. He said the Chinese Vice President will hold meetings with President Dr Arif Alvi, Prime Minister Imran Khan, and Army Chief General Qamar Javed Bajwa.

Body: 
He said an investiture ceremony will also be held in honor of Wang Qishan, where he will be conferred upon an award for his role to promote bilateral ties between Pakistan and China.

The Foreign Minister said Chinese Vice President will also visit historical places in Lahore, where Punjab Chief Minister Usman Buzdar will warmly receive the guest.

He said visit of the Chinese Vice President indicates strong ties between Islamabad and Beijing and demonstrates resolve of the two countries in developing and promoting China-Pakistan Economic Corridor.
