Title: China, Pakistan to hold month-long joint military exercise

Snippet: China and Pakistan will hold a month-long joint military exercise to boost cooperation between the two militaries and capability to jointly deal with terrorist threats including those targeting joint projects with China. According to a report, Chinese Ministry of National Defense said in a statement that a Chinese unit from a special forces brigade of the People's Liberation Army is participating in the "Warrior VII" joint exercise.
