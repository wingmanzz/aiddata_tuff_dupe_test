Title: Sports: Athletics: Five athletes to train in China

Snippet: Pakistan's five top athletes will receive training in order to get prepared for the upcoming international athletics competitions.

According to Athletics Federation of Pakistan (AFP) Secretary Mohammad Zafar, Javelin Thrower Arshad Nadeem, who has qualified for Olympics 2020, will leave for China on January 10.

Body: 
Meanwhile, Mehboob Ali, Mohammad Naeem, Uzair Rehman and Samiullah left for China from Islamabad on Friday night. All four athletes won medals in 13th South Asian Games.
