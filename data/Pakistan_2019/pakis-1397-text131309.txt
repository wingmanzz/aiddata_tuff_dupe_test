Title: Pakistan envoy asserts dialogue for Kashmir in Beijing

Snippet: The Pakistan embassy on Sunday held an opening ceremony for the student forum in Beijing, China. More than 400 students from different universities and departments attend the opening ceremony at the Islamic Republic of Pakistan Embassy.

Ambassador of Pakistan in China Masood Khalid said Pakistan released Indian pilot Abhinandan as a gesture of peace during Pulwama tensions.

Body: 
'The world appreciated Pakistan step toward peace in the region,' he said, adding only dialogue can help resolve Kashmir issue.
