Title: Programme summary from CCTV-1's Night News 1400 GMT 28 April 2019

Snippet: Summary of programme broadcast on government-controlled national broadcaster Chinese Central TV Channel 1 on 28 April at 14:00 GMT

From the "Night News" (Wan Jian Xin Wen) programme; Figures indicate programme running time.

Body: 
This is a summary of selected items from the CCTV-1 Night News programme aired at 1400-1430 gmt on 28 April 2019

The programme has used its current logo and opening computer graphics since 5 September 2011.

The announcer is Peng Kun.

1. 00:00:22 Announcer-read report over video: "Detailed Reports" -- On 28 April, Chinese President Xi Jinping delivers a speech and officiates the opening ceremony of the International Horticultural Exhibition 2019 in Beijing, China. State Council Vice Premier Hu Chunhua chairs the opening ceremony. Xi Jinping's wife Peng Liyuan attends the event. Ding Xuexiang, Yang Jiechi, Huang Kunming, and Cai Qi, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC); Wang Yi and Zhao Kezhi, councillors of the State, are also present at the event. (4 min. 36 sec.)

Xi Jinping (CCTV-1 - 2019/04/28)

2. 00:04:58 "Important Current Political News" covered in CCTV-1 "News Hookup." (filed as CHW2019042830579509; 1 min. 37 sec.)

- On 28 April, Xi Jinping meets with Djiboutian President Ismail Omar Guelleh.

- On 28 April, Xi Jinping meets with Czech President Milos Zeman.

- On 28 April, Xi Jinping meets with Tajik President Emomali Rahmon.

- On 28 April, Xi Jinping meets with Kyrgyz President Sooronbay Jeenbekov.

- On 28 April, Xi Jinping meets with Nursultan Nazarbayev, the first President in the history of Kazakhstan, and holds a ceremony at the Great Hall of the People to bestow a friendship medal to Nursultan Nazarbayev. Wang Huning attends the ceremony.

- On 28 April, Xi Jinping meets with Pakistani Prime Minister Imran Khan.

- On 28 April, Li Keqiang holds a welcome ceremony for Austrian Chancellor Sebastian Kurz. Following the welcome ceremony, Li Keqiang holds talks with Kurz.

- On 28 April, Li Keqiang separately meets with President of Swiss Confederation Ueli Maurer, Italian Prime Minister Giuseppe Conte, Cambodian Prime Minister Hun Sen, and Pakistani Prime Minister Imran Khan.

- Wang Yang recently chaired and spoke at the 21st biweekly consultation symposium of the 13th Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held in Beijing on ethnic work in cities in the new era.

- On 28 April, Wang Yang meets with Burmese State counsellor Aung San Suu Kyi.

- Wang Huning met with Cambodian Prime Minister Hun Sen on 27 April, and meets with Czech President Milos Zeman on 28 April.

- On 28 April, Zhao Leji meets with Djiboutian President Ismail Omar Guelleh.

- On 27 April, Han Zheng met with United Nations Secretary-General Antonio Guterres.

- Chinese Vice President Wang Qishan meets with Tajik President Emomali Rahmon on 28 April, and met with Pakistani Prime Minister Imran Khan on 27 April.

Wang Chen (CCTV-1 - 2019/04/28)
