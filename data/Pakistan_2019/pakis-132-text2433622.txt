Title: Dipl: (Chinese Embassy donates 4 X-Ray scanners to FBR)

Snippet: Chinese Embassy in Pakistan has donated four X-Ray scanners to the Custom Wing of Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) to prevent smuggling of contrabands especially currency.

The equipment was handed over by Counsellor of the Embassy Chen Wei to Member Customs (Operations) Dr Jawwad Uwais Agha in the presence of other senior officers of Pakistan Customs.

Body: 
The scanners having value of One million RMB, shall be instrumental in supporting the Customs authorities at the airports to effectively utilize non-intrusive technology in preventing smuggling of contrabands especially currency.

The gift of scanners will reaffirm the immutable bond of friendship between the two countries and will help promote Customs clearance facilitation in Pakistan.
