Title: Pak-China cooperation in IT sector must increase: minister

Snippet: Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Yao Jing called on Minister for Information Technology and Telecommunication Dr Khalid Maqbool Siddiqui on Tuesday.

During the meeting, matters of mutual interests and relating to IT and e-commerce were discussed, says a press release.

Body: 
The minister said that Pakistan and China were enjoying deep friendly relations and these ties will further enhance in future. He emphasised the need of boosting cooperation between Pakistan and China in IT and Telecom sector.

He said that Pakistan wanted to get benefit from Chinese experience in IT sector. The minister also sought China's help in training Pakistan's youth in IT skills besides starting youth exchange program between the two countries.

He said environment in Pakistan is conducive for Chinese companies to invest in IT and Telecom sector. The Chinese ambassador lauded the role of the Ministry of IT in the promotion of IT in the country and gave assurance for enhancing China's collaboration with Pakistan in IT sector.
