Title: Khunjerab to remain open to ensure return of traders

Snippet: The Pakistan-China border force has decided to open its border temporarily for two days to ensure the return of Pakistani traders those were trapped in Kashgar province of China. According to Pakistani and Chinese border officials, Khunjerab border will be open on December 8 and 9, during which around 100 Pakistani traders and goods-ladden containers trapped in Kashgar province of China will be released to Pakistan. It is pertinent to mention here that under the Protocol Agreement.
