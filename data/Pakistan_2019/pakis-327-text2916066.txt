Title: Info: (Learning of Chinese language to strengthen Pak-China ties: DG Radio Pakistan)

Snippet: Director General Pakistan Broadcasting Corporation Shahera Shahid has said that learning of Chinese language will be helpful to further strengthen bilateral ties between Pakistan and China.

Participating in a live program of China Radio International in Islamabad, Shahera Shahid said that the secret of China's unmatched progress is punctuality and commitment of Chinese people towards their work.

Body: 
To a question, DG PBC said the Pakistani youth have all that abilities through which they can smite the whole world but they need a clear direction.

Talking about women role in society, she said that any country in the world could not make progress without the support of women.
