Title: KP: (Pakistan, China relations to further strengthen: KP Minister)

Snippet: Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KP) Minister for Tourism and Sports Muhammad Atif Khan has said that Pakistan-China relations will be further strengthened with the passage of time.

Talking to newsmen during his visit to One Window China Center in Peshawar on Wednesday, he said establishment of the Center will provide information to Pakistani youth about China, its history, culture and scientific advancement.

Body: 
Referring to promotion of tourism in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Atif Khan said about twenty-five sites have been identified in this regard in different areas of the province.

About security situation in the province, he said it has greatly improved with the efforts of security forces.
