Title: Chinese Study Center inaugurated in UoB

Snippet: 1st Chinese Study Center has been established in University of Balochistan on Monday which has been inaugurated by Acting Deputy Ambassador Li Xian.Vice-Chancellor of UoB Dr. Javed Iqbal, academic staff and a large number of students were present in the inauguration ceremony.

Addressing the ceremony Deputy Chinese Envoy said, The Chinese Study Center would strengthen Pak-China friendly relations added I had very significance of this visit and the center would help in providing quality education to the students of Balochistan,

Body: 
'China Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) would develop Balochistan in which more skill development and educational programs would be announced.' Mr. Xian added. Addressing the ceremony Deputy Speaker NA Qasim Khan Suri said, University of Balochistan has a vital role in educating this province added despite all odds professors and teachers of UoB remained coherent and lighted education in Balochistan.

'Current Federal and Provincial Governments have been utilizing all resources to make successful CPEC and the project guarantees development for Pakistan and Balochitan.' Qasim Suri added.

Lauding the initiative VC UoB Dr. Javed Iqbal said, provincial students would be able to learn Chinese language in Chinese Study Center while BS degree program would also be announced, 'Our students would be able to visit China for scholarship and higher studies, before inaugurating Chinese Study Center, we have given full briefing to Chinese Officials regarding outcome of this center.' Dr. Javed Iqbal added.
