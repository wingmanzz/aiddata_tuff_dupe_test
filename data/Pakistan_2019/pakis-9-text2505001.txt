Title: CPEC will make Pakistan economic hub of region: Dr. Firdous

Snippet: Special Assistant to Prime Minister on Information and Broadcasting Dr Firdous Ashiq Awan says China-Pakistan Economic Corridor project will make Pakistan economic hub of the region.

Addressing a ceremony in connection with 70th anniversary of establishment of People's Republic of China in Islamabad on Monday, she said Pak-China friendship is higher than Himalaya, deeper than ocean and sweeter than honey.

Body: 
Firdous Ashiq Awan said people of Pakistan recognize China as their friend on economic, diplomatic and security fronts.

She said China is extending exemplary support to Pakistan for sensitization of youth and their education.

The Special Assistant said, under CPEC Pakistan will emerge as an economic hub in the region. She said the Chinese mega project is assurance of economic development of the whole region.

She said China and Pakistan are one nation residing in two different territories.
