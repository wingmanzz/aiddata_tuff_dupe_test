Title: Governor Balochistan visits China Study Centre in UoB

Snippet: QUETTA: Governor Balochistan Amanullah Khan Yasinzai on Sunday visited China Study Centre established in University of Balochistan called the centre conducive for CPEC. Director China Study Centre Muhammad Kamran Taj briefed Governor regarding studying activities in the centre.

"Government of Balochistan has been announcing scholarship for provincial students in China in order to enhance their knowledge and capacity following future's investment in Balochistan." Governor said stressed upon students to prepare themselves for Chinese investment in the province.

Body: 
He added, China Study Centre in UoB testifies the prolonged and strong friendship amid Pakistan and China.
