Title: Pak-China people to people contact can make things right : Faisal Javed

Snippet: Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI)'s Senator Faisal Javed Khan has said that Pakistan's alliance with China is decades old and promotion of culture between the two countries will further deepen relations at people-to-people level.

Talking to PTV news channel , He said under the credible leadership of Prime Minister Imran Khan, the PTI government is committed to channelize the China Pakistan Economic Corridor project for the betterment of the nation.

Body: 
He said Chinese vice president visit is welcoming, adding, Pakistan and China has once again reaffirmed their resolve to further strengthen all weather multi-faced bilateral ties

The visit of Chinese president to Pakistan will further strengthen Pak-China ties, he added

He urged the media for playing a very special, constructive and positive role.

Faisal said it is appreciable that both the countries are committed to safe guard each other's interests at international front.

China and Pakistan are iron brothers who support and facilitate each other in critical times, he mentioned.

Pakistan again has managed to improve its relations with other countries, adding, it is a positive development that China is sharing all of its expertise with Pakistan to stabilize Pakistan's economy.

He also mentioned that it is a good sign our both countries' youth are taking keen intrest in learning languages of each other.

He assured that CPEC project between two nation would also explore the job opportunities for Youth and it would bring a bright future for our young generation.
