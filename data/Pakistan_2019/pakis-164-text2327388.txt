Title: Adviser: (Govt fully focused on promoting ease of doing business in country: Dawood)

Snippet: Adviser to Prime Minister on Commerce, Textile, Industry and Production and Investment Abdul Razak Dawood has said that the government is fully focused on promoting ease of doing business in the country.

Talking to Chinese Ambassador Yao Jing here on Thursday, he said additional investment by the Chinese company in Pakistan is a heartening step.

Body: 
During the meeting, matters pertaining to bolster trade and investment came under discussion.
