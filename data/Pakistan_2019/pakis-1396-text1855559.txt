Title: Pak and China to sign preliminary design of ML-1 project during upcoming visit of PM Imran Khan to Beijing

Snippet: Pakistan and China will sign the preliminary design of ML-1 project during the upcoming visit of Prime Minister Imran Khan to Beijing.

This was stated by Minister for Railways Sheikh Rashid Ahmed while talking to Vice-Governor of China's province Hubei, Huang Chuping in Islamabad on Friday.

Body: 
The Minister for Railways said completion of Peshawar to Karachi Main Line 1 project will bring revolution of rail in Pakistan. He said the upcoming visit of Prime Minister Imran Khan to China is very significant one which will help made significant progress in CPEC related projects.

He invited Hubei's provincial government to become partner in Gwadar-Quetta-Taftan ML-II rail project.

The Vice-Governor of China's province Hubei said provincial government of Hubei is interested in partnership with Pakistan Railways. He said the projects of China Pakistan Economic Corridor have ushered in a golden era between the two countries.

On this occasion, the Vice-Governor of China's province Hubei also extended special invitation to visit his province. The Minister for Railways accepted the invitation.
